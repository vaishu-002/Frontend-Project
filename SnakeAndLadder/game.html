Welcome to Bali 🌺

The Island Paradise for Sun, Surf & Culture


Bali is known for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, beaches, and coral reefs. It’s a top destination for travelers who want to enjoy nature, rich culture, and vibrant nightlife.

Here’s everything you need to explore and love this beautiful island!

  • Language: Indonesian
  • Currency: Indonesian Rupiah (Rp)
  • Time Zone: GMT +8

  4. Sacred Monkey Forest 🐒
  5. Kuta Beach 🏖️
